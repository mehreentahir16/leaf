Leaf

A Benchmark for Federated Settings


Leaf is a benchmarking framework for learning in federated settings. Current datasets include FEMNIST, Sentiment140 and Shakespeare. For a possible canonical application of Leaf, check out the Google AI Blog post on federated learning.
